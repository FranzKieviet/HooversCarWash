About Us

“From Grime to Shine" It is our slogan… It is our belief that our company, Hoover Car Wash, built from the ground up stands on and stands behind. In the heart of Los Angeles, established in 1950, Hoover Car Wash marks a place for everyone to be a part of the Hoovers family, whether they drive by to pick up one of our munchies, hang out, or watch the Chivas and the Lakers. Our sole mission doesn’t stop at providing an immaculate, pristine car, but also a spotless experience serving you. We are committed to move past the traditional, classic car wash towards maximizing satisfaction with our outstanding service and quality cleaning utilizing our cutting-edge technology, making every driver that drives out of Hoover Car Wash, have a memorable experience.
